FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS


  • What is the purpose of this tool?

    This tool has been developed as part of a master's thesis project to predict the potential jumping height of offspring when two specific horses are bred together. It aims to provide valuable insights for horse breeders and enthusiasts.

  • How accurate are the predictions from this tool?

    The predictions provided by this tool are based on advanced algorithms and available data. While efforts have been made to ensure accuracy, it's important to understand that breeding involves multiple factors, and the actual results may vary.

  • What horse breeds does this tool support?

    This tool is designed to work with various horse breeds, making it a versatile resource for breeders across different equestrian disciplines.

  • How can I input data for accurate predictions?

    To obtain precise jump height predictions, you'll need to enter detailed information about both parent horses, including their breed, pedigree, performance records, and any genetic testing data if available.

  • Is my data and privacy protected?

    Your data privacy is a priority for us. All information provided is encrypted and securely stored, adhering to best practices. We do not share your data with third parties.

  • Is there any cost associated with using this tool?

    As part of the thesis project, this tool is available for free to users. There are no usage fees or subscriptions required.

  • Can I use this tool for commercial breeding purposes?

    Absolutely! This tool can be used for both hobbyist and commercial breeding. It offers valuable insights for making informed breeding decisions.

  • How often is the tool updated?

    As part of the thesis project, the tool's database will be updated periodically to incorporate the latest genetic research and performance data, ensuring accuracy and relevancy.

  • How do I access this tool for my breeding project?

    To access the tool, simply visit the website and follow the instructions for inputting the necessary information. It's user-friendly and designed to be accessible to all horse enthusiasts.

  • Where can I get further assistance or support for using the tool?

    For any questions or assistance related to using the tool, you can reach out to the developer through the provided contact details on the website. Your feedback and queries are valuable for refining the tool's performance.
